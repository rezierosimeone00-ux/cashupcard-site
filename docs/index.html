How Cashup Card works

Instant Uplift Cards

Each Drop or QR release comes with a limited number of cards per cluster, making access exclusive and time-sensitive.

Join the waitlist to get the app link first. At launch, unlock extra value via geo-segmented Drops or by scanning a QR code at participating locations in your area. After you purchase your card, you’ll receive two in-app value pop-ups automatically — no drop or QR required — so your uplift will be multiplied.

Real-Time Checkout Value

Use your Cashup Card and see the uplift apply instantly at checkout. No waiting for cashback, no chasing coupons — your balance reflects the bonus the moment you pay.

That extra value can also be spent fractionally across multiple merchants in the same category — grocery or pet — turning every purchase into a bigger deal.

For Local Businesses

Drops are capped within each cluster, so stores see steady, manageable foot traffic without the risk of overflow.

Connecting is simple through APIs, while Cashup’s AI does the heavy lifting. Clusters (H3 res 5/6) balance local population with store categories, so users spend nearby and shops gain predictable traffic.

No upfront cost: Cashup covers all expenses until a card is redeemed in-store. At that moment, a small commission applies, tied to the uplift value received by the shopper.

AI-Powered Platform

Cashup blends financial innovation with advanced AI. Virtual cards let users load less and spend more, while businesses onboard with no upfront fees.

AI clusters balance users and merchants for efficient growth. The Advertising Automation Engine fine-tunes campaigns in real time.

Built-in fraud detection keeps transactions safe. Live analytics improve performance, and a modular architecture makes scaling fast. Onboarding takes minutes, redemption takes seconds — Cashup moves quickly from pilot to market.

Local Growth Engine

Cashup builds engagement where it matters most — everyday local shopping. AI-driven clusters connect users directly with nearby stores.

Each card strengthens participation: people access real value, stores grow without risk. By keeping value local, Cashup creates lasting engagement that benefits both communities and commerce.

Future Vision

Cashup Card starts with a pilot focused on essentials, but the platform is designed to scale.

The same AI that links users to local shops can expand nationally and internationally, adapting instantly to new markets and behaviors.

From prepaid cards to full financial services, Cashup is long-term infrastructure — evolving with people, businesses, and communities.
